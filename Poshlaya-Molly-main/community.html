My Poshlaya Molly Experience

I first started listening to Poshlaya Molly in 2017, and they are still my favorite band to this day. What drew me to their music was something unique – their style is unlike anything I had heard before. Their songs span across various genres, and no matter the mood, there's always a track that fits.

I absolutely love their music, not just for the sound but for the raw emotion they convey. It's something I never knew I was missing in my playlists. Over the years, I’ve even started collecting their merch, and I can’t wait for their concert in Finland. I’m sure it’s going to be an unforgettable experience!
