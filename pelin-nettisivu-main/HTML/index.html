Hey there!

Your parents want you to know the traffic rules — but you couldn’t care less about learning them and just want to play with your friends? Then we’ve got the perfect option for you! You and your parents are both going to love it!

Nostalgy School Run is the game where kids can play freely while learning about traffic and safety equipment at the same time. This way, both parents and children are happy — and the roads become safer, too.

On this website, you’ll get to explore our game, meet its characters, get to know the developers behind it, and even learn about our partner. Stay tuned and don’t forget to check out our latest updates in the News section!

Game Features:

Endless Challenges
Dodge speeding cars, avoid reckless cyclists, and navigate unpredictable pedestrians as you race through an ever-changing environment based on real-life traffic scenarios from Tampere.
Learn Traffic Rules
Every obstacle is a lesson! From crossing at zebra crossings to obeying traffic lights, players will pick up essential traffic safety rules while having fun.
Pixel-Perfect Graphics
Immerse yourself in charming retro-style pixel art that brings the chaotic streets to life.
Dynamic Gameplay
The further you run, the faster it gets! Stay sharp as new challenges appear to test your skills.

Our Characters

Kiddo - the fastest kid on the block! Dodging cars, jumping over potholes, and outrunning chaos, all while trying to make it to school on time.

Nopsu – the fastest cat in town, so quick you’ll barely see him coming! He’s the master of stealth, slipping through the shadows and vanishing in the blink of an eye. Whether it’s swiping treats or darting around corners, Nopsu is always a step ahead.

Mummimamma - she might look like she’s taking a nap, but don’t be fooled! When our hero tries to race through, Granny’s and her walking stick always in the way. Getting past Granny is no easy task, but watch out – she’s quicker than she looks!
